## 1. Right of Withdrawal (EU Consumer Rights)

### 1.1 14-Day Cooling-Off Period

In accordance with EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU, consumers have the right to withdraw from a contract within 14 days without giving any reason.

**However, this right does NOT apply when:**
- The service has been fully performed with your prior express consent
- You have expressly agreed that the service may begin before the end of the 14-day withdrawal period
- The service is for a specific date or period (e.g., scheduled pet sitting on a particular date)

### 1.2 Waiver of Withdrawal Right

By booking a pet care service with a specific date and time, you expressly:
- Request that the service begins immediately
- Acknowledge that you will lose your right of withdrawal once the service is fully performed
- Agree that the service is time-sensitive and cannot be postponed

## 2. Cancellation Policy

### 2.1 Cancellation by Pet Owner

#### More than 48 hours before service:
- **Full refund** (100% of the booking amount)
- No cancellation fee
- Refund processed within 5-10 business days

#### 24-48 hours before service:
- **50% refund** of the booking amount
- 50% cancellation fee retained to compensate the sitter
- Refund processed within 5-10 business days

#### Less than 24 hours before service:
- **No refund**
- Full amount retained to compensate the sitter for lost opportunity
- Exception: Emergency situations (see Section 2.4)

#### After service has started:
- **No refund**
- Service is considered delivered

### 2.2 Cancellation by Pet Sitter

If a sitter cancels a confirmed booking:

#### More than 24 hours before service:
- **Full refund** (100%) to the pet owner
- No penalty to pet owner
- Platform may penalize the sitter

#### Less than 24 hours before service:
- **Full refund** (100%) to the pet owner
- **€10 compensation credit** for inconvenience
- Sitter may face account restrictions

### 2.3 Cancellation by Platform

The Platform reserves the right to cancel bookings in cases of:
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity
- Safety concerns
- Force majeure events

**In such cases:**
- Full refund issued to pet owner
- No penalty to either party (unless violation occurred)

### 2.4 Emergency Cancellations

In case of genuine emergencies (medical, family, natural disasters), we may offer:
- Full or partial refund at our discretion
- Rescheduling options
- Case-by-case evaluation

**To request emergency cancellation:**
- Contact support immediately
- Provide documentation if possible
- Explain the circumstances

## 3. Refund Process

### 3.1 How Refunds Work

1. **Request:** Submit cancellation through the Platform
2. **Review:** We review the request within 24 hours
3. **Approval:** Eligible refunds are approved automatically
4. **Processing:** Refund initiated to original payment method
5. **Receipt:** Funds appear in 5-10 business days

### 3.2 Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** used for booking:
- Credit/Debit card: 5-10 business days
- Bank transfer: 3-7 business days
- PayPal: 1-3 business days

### 3.3 Platform Fees

- **Service fee:** Non-refundable in all cases
- **Booking amount:** Refundable according to cancellation policy
- **Processing fees:** May be deducted from refund amount

### 4.1 Service Not Provided

If a sitter fails to provide the booked service:
- **Full refund** (100%)

- Provide evidence if available

### 4.2 Service Quality Issues

If the service was provided but not as described:
- Contact support within 24 hours
- Provide detailed explanation and evidence
- Partial refund may be offered (case-by-case)
- Mediation between parties

### 4.3 Dispute Resolution

1. **Direct Communication:** Try to resolve with the other party first
2. **Platform Mediation:** Contact support for assistance
3. **Evidence Review:** Provide photos, messages, or other proof
4. **Decision:** Platform makes final decision within 5 business days
5. **Appeal:** You may appeal the decision within 7 days

## 5. No-Show Policy

### 5.1 Pet Owner No-Show

If you fail to make your pet available for the scheduled service:
- **No refund**
- Sitter receives full payment
- Considered a completed booking

### 5.2 Sitter No-Show

If a sitter doesn't show up for a confirmed booking:
- **Full refund** (100%)
- **€10 compensation credit**
- Sitter may face account suspension
